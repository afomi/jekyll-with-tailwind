A simple, modern template for building your own website. No subscriptions, no platforms, no algorithms. Just your content, your design, your rules.

Why build your own?

Take back control of your online presence

Social platforms come and go. Algorithms change. Your website is yours forever.

Your Domain, Your Identity
Build on your own domain. No platform branding, no algorithm deciding who sees your content. Your online home, on your terms.
Simple & Modern Stack
Jekyll for static site generation, Tailwind CSS for styling. Fast, secure, and easy to customize. No databases, no complexity.
Free to Host
Deploy to GitHub Pages for free, or use any static host. No monthly fees, no vendor lock-in. Just HTML, CSS, and your content.
Fully Customizable
Custom colors, typography, and layouts. Check out the design system to see what's possible.

Ready to build your own?

Fork this template, customize it, and deploy. Your website can be live in minutes.
